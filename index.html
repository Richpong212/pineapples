About Us

Rocks Pineapples is one of the leading company from Costa Rica. We produce the best quality pineapples. Our team is geared toward procuding the best pineapples. Our motives is quality over quantity.We cultivate the golden pineapple, also known as MD2 variety. We currently count with nearly 700 cultivated hectares, 1.730 acres, and the capacity of expansion. To guarantee the quality of our development, our packaging plant follows the more strict safety and quality rules that the process demands, supported by the Global GAP certification.

Smooth Cayenne

One of our produced Pineapple is Smooth Cayenne. It has low fiber. Mainly, it is prized for canning, having sufficient fiber for firm slices and cubes as well as excellent flavor. They have good for making fruit juice.It has rich midly acidic flavor

This is one of the pineapples,that is somewhat softer than most other pineapple varieties. It has a unique vibrant greem color.Sugarloaf pineapples are an excellent source of manganese, and a good source of potassium, calcium, vitamin C and fiber.
